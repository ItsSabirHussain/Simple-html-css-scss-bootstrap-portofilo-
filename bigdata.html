BIG Data

The concept of data has become very vast in the past few years. It has now become extremely important to save and store the data. Enterprises have now started to store their extremely large data sets which are termed as big data. However, big data is now considered as the solution to the problem where the data has a lot of volume, high velocity, and high veracity and comes from various resources.
